Everything you need to know about using the AI Question Generator

Getting Started

Welcome to the AI Question Generator! This powerful tool transforms any text into intelligent questions using advanced Natural Language Processing. Follow these simple steps to get started:

  1. Enter your text in the input box or upload a document (TXT or PDF format)
  2. Click the "Generate Questions" button to start the AI analysis
  3. Wait for the AI to process your content (usually takes 10-30 seconds)
  4. Review the generated questions displayed in organized cards
  5. Use the action buttons to copy, download, or regenerate questions

Supported File Formats

Our AI Question Generator supports multiple input methods to give you maximum flexibility:

Direct Text Input

Type or paste text directly into the input field. Perfect for quick question generation.

TXT Files

Upload plain text files (.txt) containing your content for processing.

PDF Documents

Upload PDF files (.pdf) and we'll extract the text automatically.

File Size Limit: Maximum 10MB per file. For best results, ensure your text is clear, well-formatted, and contains substantial content (minimum 100 characters recommended).

Troubleshooting

Encountering issues? Here are solutions to common problems:

  • No questions generated: Ensure your text has at least 100 characters and contains meaningful content
  • Upload failed: Check file format (only .txt and .pdf supported) and size (max 10MB)
  • Processing timeout: Try with shorter text or refresh the page and try again
  • Poor question quality: Use well-structured, clear text for better results
  • Copy function not working: Your browser needs clipboard permissions - check browser settings
  • Mobile display issues: Refresh the page or try in landscape mode

Frequently Asked Questions

How many questions can I generate?

+

There's no limit! Generate as many questions as you need. The AI typically produces 3-8 high-quality questions per text, depending on content length and complexity.

Is my data stored or shared?

+

No! Your text and generated questions are processed in real-time and not stored on our servers. We respect your privacy and don't share any data with third parties.

What makes a good text for question generation?

+

Texts with clear facts, proper names, specific details, and well-structured sentences work best. Avoid overly technical jargon or very short passages for optimal results.
